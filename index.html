You are cordially invited to the

Musician's Ball

An Evening in Old Hollywood

The Gamma Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi, together with the Husky Marching Band, invites all musicians associated with the University of Washington to join us on May 16, 2015 for the first ever Musician's Ball! Doors open at the Don James Center at 7 PM.

The Musician's Ball is a magical night of music, dancing and dining. Come dressed in your best black-tie attire.

Guests are welcome. Tickets are 22 dollars.

Featuring The Touch of Brass

The Touch of Brass is a old-style brass band, featuring a number of current Husky Band musicians. Formed in 2012 under the moniker, "The Husky Horns", the band specializes in second-line New Orleans music, with elements of funk, jazz and rock.
