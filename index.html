Welcome

The Gamma Chapter of Kappa Kappa Psi will once again be hosting a Musician's Ball! Come enjoy an evening of food, music, and dancing with us! All musicians associated with the University of Washington are invited to join us on May 21st, 2017.

The theme is Awards Show, so dress accordingly!

Tickets are $15 for 1 and $25 for 2. Doors will open at 7, with food being served at 7:30!
